Our Story

A journey of passion, tradition, and the pursuit of the perfect cup.

From Passion to Purpose

Ultra Tea was born in 2015 from a simple yet profound realization: in our fast-paced world, we had lost the art of slowing down. Our founder, after years in the corporate world, discovered the transformative power of tea during a sabbatical in the hills of Darjeeling.

What began as a personal quest for the perfect cup evolved into a mission to share this experience with the world. We spent two years traveling to tea gardens across Asia and Africa, forging relationships with family-owned farms that shared our commitment to quality and organic farming practices.

Today, Ultra Tea is more than a brand—it's a community of tea lovers united by a belief that every cup should be a moment of mindfulness, a pause in the chaos, a reminder to breathe. All our products are 100% organic, sourced from certified organic farms to ensure the purest, most natural tea experience.

From Leaf to Cup

Every Ultra Tea blend goes through a meticulous journey to ensure exceptional quality.

Step 01

Sourcing

We partner directly with certified organic farms in India, China, Japan, and Kenya. Our tea masters visit each garden personally to select only the finest leaves.

Step 02

Harvesting

Leaves are hand-picked during optimal seasons—typically early spring for green teas and summer for robust black varieties. This ensures peak flavor and nutrient content.

Step 03

Processing

Using traditional methods passed down through generations, our leaves are carefully withered, rolled, and dried. Each tea type requires a unique process to unlock its character.

Step 04

Quality Testing

Every batch undergoes rigorous testing for purity, flavor profile, and NAFDAC compliance. Only teas that meet our exacting standards make it to your cup.

Step 05

Packaging

Sealed in eco-friendly, biodegradable packaging within hours of processing to preserve freshness. Our pouches are designed to protect against light, air, and moisture.

100% Organic, Pure and Natural

At Ultra Tea, organic isn't just a label—it's our promise. Every leaf in our blends comes from certified organic farms that follow strict guidelines to ensure no pesticides, herbicides, or synthetic fertilizers touch our tea.

Certified Organic: All our teas are certified organic by recognized authorities, guaranteeing the highest standards of purity and quality.

Natural Processing: We use traditional methods that preserve the natural flavors and health benefits of our organic leaves, from hand-picking to careful drying.

Quality Assurance: Rigorous testing ensures every batch meets our organic standards, delivering the cleanest, most authentic tea experience.
